Submit Your Dream for a Reading

Welcome to my form—let's get acquainted!

To get started, fill out your information below. When describing your dream, please provide as much detail as you can remember and be sure to include any memories of how you felt during the dream in the spaces provided. However, if there are parts that you can't remember, don't worry!

Just write down what you do remember, and if there's a blank spot, just jot down that something happened there but you don't know what it is, and then continue on to the next part that you remember. This helps me connect with both you and the dream to help provide the most detailed reading!
